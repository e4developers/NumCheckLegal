Privacy Policy for NumCheck

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Introduction

NumCheck ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our lottery ticket checker mobile application.

NumCheck is designed with privacy in mind. Your lottery ticket numbers never leave your device. All ticket data is stored locally using your device's built-in storage, and we do not operate any servers that receive or store your personal information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Lottery Ticket Numbers: Numbers you manually enter are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • App Preferences: Settings such as theme choice, notification preferences, jackpot alert thresholds, and game type selections are stored locally on your device.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data collected by Google AdMob when ads are displayed (see Third-Party Services below).
  • Network Metadata: When the app fetches lottery draw results, your device's IP address is visible to our API proxy service (Cloudflare) as part of normal HTTPS traffic. No personal data is transmitted in these requests.

How We Use Your Information

We use information only to:

  • Check your lottery tickets against official draw results — entirely on your device
  • Send local win notifications when your numbers match a draw (if you enable this)
  • Display lottery statistics, hot and cold numbers, and draw history — using cached draw data on your device
  • Calculate your winnings and spending history — entirely from data stored locally on your device
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Remind you of upcoming draws (local notifications only)

Data Storage

  • Local Only: All lottery ticket data, preferences, and statistics are stored on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not back up, sync, or store your ticket numbers on any server.
  • Cached Draw Results: Official lottery draw results are temporarily cached on your device to reduce network requests. This cache contains only publicly available draw numbers — no personal data.
  • No Account Required: NumCheck requires no login, account, or email address to use.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display banner and native advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect and use:

  • Advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • General location (country/region level, not precise GPS)

AdMob operates under Google's privacy framework. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

For more information: Google Privacy Policy

Cloudflare (API Proxy)

Lottery draw result requests are routed through a Cloudflare Worker we operate. Cloudflare acts as a secure intermediary between the app and the lottery data provider. Cloudflare processes network traffic (including IP addresses) in accordance with their privacy policy. No personal data or ticket information is included in these requests — only requests for publicly available lottery draw results.

For more information: Cloudflare Privacy Policy

LotteryData.io

Official lottery draw results are sourced from LotteryData.io via our Cloudflare proxy. Only requests for publicly available draw result data are made. No personal information is sent to this service.

Notifications

NumCheck may send two types of local notifications, both of which are processed entirely on your device:

  • Win Notifications: Sent when your entered ticket numbers match a winning draw. Requires your permission and can be disabled at any time in Settings.
  • Draw Reminders: Morning reminders sent the day after each draw. Requires your permission and can be disabled at any time in Settings.

No notification content is sent to or processed by our servers.

Children's Privacy

NumCheck is not intended for use by anyone under 18 years of age (or the legal lottery age in your jurisdiction, whichever is higher). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

Data Retention

  • Ticket Data: Retained locally until you manually delete tickets or uninstall the app.
  • Cached Draw Results: Refreshed periodically; stale cache is automatically replaced.
  • App Preferences: Retained locally until you change them or uninstall the app.
  • Ad Data: Managed by Google AdMob per Google's data retention policies.

Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All ticket data is visible directly within the app.
  • Delete: Delete individual tickets or all tickets at any time from within the app.
  • Notifications: Enable or disable win notifications and draw reminders in Settings at any time.
  • Ad Personalization: Opt out of personalized ads via your device's advertising settings.
  • Full Removal: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data from your device.

Data Security

  • Ticket numbers are stored only on your device and are never transmitted externally.
  • All network requests (for draw results) use HTTPS encryption.
  • Our Cloudflare proxy prevents direct exposure of the underlying data provider's API credentials.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable law. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
