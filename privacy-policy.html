# Privacy Policy for NumCheck **Last Updated: January 16, 2026** ## Introduction NumCheck ("the App"), owned and operated by E4D ("we", "our", or "us"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our lottery ticket checker mobile application. ## Information We Collect ### Information You Provide - **Lottery Tickets**: Numbers you manually enter to check against draw results - **App Preferences**: Settings like notification preferences and game type selections ### Automatically Collected Information - **Usage Data**: App interactions, features used, and session duration - **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version - **Advertising Data**: Ad interactions collected by Google AdMob (see Third-Party Services below) ## How We Use Your Information We use the collected information to: - Check your lottery tickets against official draw results - Send you win notifications (if enabled) - Display relevant advertisements - Improve app functionality and user experience - Comply with legal obligations ## Data Storage - **Local Storage**: All lottery ticket data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences - **No Cloud Sync**: We do not store your ticket numbers on our servers - **Cache**: Draw results are temporarily cached on your device to reduce API calls ## Third-Party Services ### Google AdMob We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect: - Device identifiers - Ad interaction data - General location (country/region) For more information: [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) ### LotteryData.io API We fetch official lottery draw results from LotteryData.io. Only draw result data is retrieved; we do not send any personal information to this service. ## Notifications If you enable win notifications: - We will send local push notifications when your tickets match winning numbers - You can disable notifications at any time in Settings - Notifications are processed locally on your device ## Children's Privacy NumCheck is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. ## Data Retention - **Ticket Data**: Stored locally until you delete tickets or uninstall the app - **Cached Draw Results**: Refreshed every hour; old cache is automatically deleted - **Preferences**: Stored until you change them or uninstall the app ## Your Rights You have the right to: - **Access**: View all data stored locally through the app - **Delete**: Remove any tickets you've entered - **Opt-Out**: Disable win notifications - **Uninstall**: Remove the app and all associated local data ## Data Security We implement reasonable security measures: - All data is stored locally on your device - No transmission of ticket numbers to external servers - Secure HTTPS connections for API calls ## Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
